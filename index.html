Free

Edugle is a free e-learning platform dedicated to providing quality education for IIT JEE preparation, offering comprehensive question practice and mentorship sessions. It aims to guide aspirants through the journey of excelling in JEE Mains & Advance, supporting them in achieving their dream of qualifying one of the toughest exams and securing admission to the best colleges through counseling assistance.

About Us

Initiative

Edugle was founded with a pioneering mission: to offer high-quality, accessible education to all students aiming to shape their futures by succeeding in prestigious national-level undergraduate exams such as JEE Mains & Advance and NEET. This visionary initiative embodies a commitment to democratize education, ensuring that every aspiring student, regardless of background, has the opportunity to excel in these critical exams. By providing free and exceptional educational resources, Edugle empowers students to overcome barriers and realize their potential, fostering a brighter and more equitable future for all.

About Us

Edugle is a free e-learning platform which aims at providing free and quality education to students preparing for the IIT JEE examination. With a motto of free education for all we are dedicated towards supporting the aspirants in their journey to qualify the examination with flying colours. Edugle boosts your preparation of JEE Mains & Advance through various levels of question practice which ultimately helps you move closer towards your dream of cracking one of the toughest exams, which is the IIT JEE. We help students through various mentorship sessions and also guide them with their councelling in order to grab the best college for themselves.

Working

Initially, our approach involved disseminating study materials to students through multiple online channels. As time progressed, our methodology evolved. We transitioned from merely providing study resources to actively engaging with students. We introduced problem-solving sessions, assisting them in tackling complex questions. Additionally, during counseling sessions, we extended our support by offering guidance tailored to individual needs. This transformation reflects our commitment to not only delivering content but also fostering a comprehensive learning experience. Our journey signifies the progression from passive material dissemination to active problem-solving facilitation, exemplifying our dedication to student success.

Results

Our journey has been defined by relentless dedication and unwavering effort, propelling us to our current position. The immense hard work invested has yielded remarkable outcomes. Our students' achievements in diverse entrance examinations have been a source of immense pride and validation. Their exceptional results stand as a testament to our commitment and the effectiveness of our educational approach. These achievements fuel our determination to continue fostering excellence, pushing boundaries, and empowering more students to achieve their dreams through our steadfast efforts.

FAQ

Why should I choose EDUGLE?

There are alot of new mentorship startups. They will charge you for mentorship but we donot charge a single rupee for any type of help. We help students in solving their doubts, guiding them, mentoring them, helping them in councelling and providing them best opportunity to connect with the students of different IIT/NIT/IIIT/GFTI before going them to college.

Why should I trust EDUGLE team?

Our team contains those people only who are there in IITs/NITs/IIITs/GFTIs and got these colleges in recent year ranging from 2020 to 2023. So they know how to prepare for JEE and how to tackle the problems that you are facing during your JEE preparation

How can I access the Edugle study material?

The study material can be accessed through our Website free of cost.The questions are well-organised and graded to provide a seamless experience to our students.

Is the question bank free completely?

Yes, Our question bank is absolutely free and non-chargeable at any stage. However, a dedicated team filters and sorts questions from across resources to provide you with comprehensive material,chapterwise.

Where are the Edugle Headquarters?

We have a team of dedicated individuals passionate about our vision from across the country, currently in reputed institutes, so we operate remotely which , ensures no delay in the deliverables and helps us constantly improve our quality.

How can I contact Edugle team for counselling mentorship?

You can mail us at Our Official Mail or can fill the EDUGLE Councelling Helpdesk form. Please give the correct details so that our team can connect with you. We also ensure that our team wwill connect with you within 24-48hr.

Will Edugle charge me for guidance in my state counselling?

Edugle realises the crucial need for guidance during counselling, and so we provide complete guidance for any counselling, free of cost. Also, we ensure personal attention to each student, based on their priorities and preferences

I wish to seek guidance for my counselling but don't wish to disclose my rank in public, what should I do?

We, at Edugle, ensure complete transparency and confidentiality of the personal information of any of our students. All guidance provided for the JEE counselling is for the access of the respective student and their mentor only.
